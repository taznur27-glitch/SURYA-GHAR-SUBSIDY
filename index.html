Essential Surya Ghar FAQ

What is the subsidy for a 3kW solar system?

As of the April 2026 MNRE guidelines, the **Surya Ghar Solar Subsidy** is ₹30,000 per kW for the first 2kW and ₹18,000 for the 3rd kW. For any system 3kW or larger, the total subsidy is capped at **₹78,000**.

How much space is required for a 3kW system?

You require approximately **300 square feet** of shade-free rooftop area. Modern N-Type TopCon panels used in 2026 can often reduce this requirement to 250-270 sq. ft.
